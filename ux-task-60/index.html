UX Milestone 60: Capstone

60. UX Case Study: Optimizing Checkout Friction

Author: UX Lead | Duration: 6-Week Design Sprint

01

The Executive Problem Statement

User research loops (Task 51) and usability metrics (Task 55) revealed that **62% of online shoppers abandoned their carts** specifically at the payment selection node. Unexpected added layout steps and opaque final pricing architectures were causing major user drop-offs.

02

The Design Strategy & Methodology

Using structural empathy mapping benchmarks (Task 53), we streamlined the information architecture (Task 57) to completely eliminate redundant manual account input boxes. We deployed an optimized, single-layered checkout interaction flow path (Task 58).

✓

Measurable Validation Results

Post-launch usability testing protocols validated outstanding optimization jumps across global checkout funnels:

  • +42% Growth in baseline conversion success metrics.
  • -28 Seconds Saved in average end-to-end task completion times.
  • 0% Drop-offs caused by pricing transparency issues.
